Hi, I'm Paru Banjara.

I am an Information Analysis (IA) student in Bachelor of Science in Information (BSI) program at the University of Michigan. Besides coding, I also have a great passion of exploring in a wide variety of domains, such as design, artworks, photography and travelling.

To talk about my past experiences, I have worked at different hospitals in Nepal as a student nurse. I have worked directly with patients, clients, community members, and health care teams. I have worked with the patients in the most critical situations, such as patient with burn, fracture vertebra, post-operative patient, and patient in labor and delivery.

Working as a student nurse, I not only learned a lot of health care related skills and knowledge, but also gained other soft skills, such as working in a team, building interpersonal relationships with people from different domains and backgrounds, working under a stressful situation and acting accordingly, and providing counseling and health guidance to patients and clients.

This image shows me working on making a clay pot for my school project while I was in the nursing program. This project was about conducting an exibition on the topics related to the health of children from different age groups. For this project, my team and I were assigned to work on the topic called weaning.

During my free time, I like to explore around by going for a walk in the woods, picking wild berries, and looking for wild mushrooms.
